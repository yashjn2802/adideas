Welcome to Ad Group

Leading the insdustry since decades

Since 1983, Ad group is one of the pioneers in providing outdoor advertising, publicity and OOH material fabrication solutions throughout India. We are one of its kind multispecialty promotion and publicity service providers offering most exceptional advertising/printing /fabrication/photography/services.

The company is managed by Mr Atul Dalmia and his elderly able son Mr Mridul Dalmia who are running the organization with a set of experienced and professional team members and resources. With Our 38 years of vast experience, we strongly believe in the Motto “Release your potential” since we since we understand the requirement of our clients and provide them business solution that matters and benefit them in the long run.

Our strong foothold in the industry has made us the proven experts as w e have emerged as pioneers for providing customized solutions in the feild of outdoor advertising and bring revolution in the signage industry .With our own fabrication set up which is equipped with routers, Lazer, fiber lazer, waterjet, channel bending and profile cutting Hi end modern technology machines including large format digital printing setup which comprehend of solvent/eco-solvent/latex and UV printing ... We have a diverse portfolio of products ranging from all types of ATL/BTL/TTL/Digital Advertising.All types of customised printing, cutting and engraving on any surface with specialization in coated SS Sheets/Angles& Channels and Korean temples.

With a group turnover of Approx 100 Crore we have recently come up with our Croma Setup digital studio wherein we have plans to promote child and female talent through e-commerce platform. We have also started manufacturing of immunity booster products which we are planning to launch by June 2020 end

Our digital market division has been serving more than 50 satisfied clients for last 2 years. One can visit our showroom cum studio to have a look at the varieties of products and services under one roof.

In future we have plans to launch an E- Portal for Indian Occults. We have our own NGO in the name of Mercy & Vision through which we are trying to disburse our CRS to the maximum people.
